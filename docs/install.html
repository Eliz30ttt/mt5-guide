MT5 Install Guide & First Trade Tutorial

Step-by-step instructions to get MT5 installed and place your first demo trade safely.

Step 1 — Download MT5

  • Desktop: Visit MetaTrader 5 official site and download for Windows or Mac.
  • Mobile: Search “MetaTrader 5” on Google Play or App Store and install.

Step 2 — Install MT5

  • Run the installer (desktop) or open the app (mobile).
  • Follow instructions to complete installation.
  • Launch MT5 after installation.

Step 3 — Open a Demo Account

  • Click “File → Open an Account” (desktop) or “Settings → New Account” (mobile).
  • Choose a broker from Broker Comparison Page.
  • Fill in demo account details and save your login info.

Step 4 — Explore Charts & Place Your First Trade

  • Open “Market Watch” to see currency pairs.
  • Right-click a pair → “New Order” to place a demo trade.
  • Use stop-loss and take-profit settings to manage risk.
  • Track trades in the “Trade” tab.

Step 5 — Learn & Practice

  • Spend a few days trading demo funds to understand MT5 tools.
  • Review charts, experiment with indicators, and check order execution.
